How to play

This is a "Who wants to be a Millionaire" style quiz game, focusing on the topic of NFL football.

1. There will be ten questions of varying difficulty. You will select the best answer from a list of four to move onto the next question.

2. You have two lifelines. You can only use them one time each. The 50/50 cuts the available answers in half. A hint will be provided when it is selected. You may not use both on the same question.

3. Simply double click the answer and you will receive feedback on whether it's correct or incorrect.

4. You must get all the questions correct to win! When answered incorrectly the game will start over.

So, Are you ready?
